6 Reasons You'll Love SBT


1. SBT is Blazingly Fast!

SBT is really fast – and it will REMAIN fast. We are running live, 24-hour-a-day benchmark testing to guarantee consistent code performance. Missiles get launched if it drops under 97/100 (I’m serious).

2. SBT Loves Mobile

Mobile friendliness isn’t just a nice thing – it now affects your Pankrank and that means traffic and money. SBT is rated at 100/100 on Google’s Mobile Testing tool. If it ever fails, there are consequences. (*See below.) 

3. SBT ALWAYS Follows Best Practice Coding

Constant Theme Check benchmarking ensures SBT always adheres to the latest WordPress standards and practices. The perfect platform for your ideas.

SBT is Easy to Use

SBT fully supports a range of visual theme builders out-of-the-box including Divi Builder, Beaver Builder, Visual Page Composer and more.

5. SBT Loves WooCommerce

Plug in ecommerce ability with ease WooCommerce powers 37% the world’s online stores. SBT makes adding ecommerce to your theme a breeze.

6. SBT is Proper Open Source GPL

There is no fancy 'lawyer-speak' licensing here. Open Source has always been at the heart of Wordpress – we put it at the heart of SBT too. Take it, use it, give good stuff back to the community.
